About TASZ

The turkish students association's goal is to hold events to celebrate the rich turkish culture and to connect students of all backgrounds. Get to know new people, learn more about turkish culture, it's history and it's peoples' values and join discussions. Furthermore, TASZ aims to highlight people of interest such as artists, historians, professors and the like, inviting them as speakers to Zurich and introducing them to interested students for networking.
Another goal TASZ holds is to be a landing point for Turkish students who moved to Zurich alone and might benefit from guidance to student life in Zurich. With the aid of TASZ a warm and entertaining environment might be created, where students can make new experiences together, enjoy Turkish culture and make new friends.
